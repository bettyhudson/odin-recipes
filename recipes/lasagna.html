Lasagna Recipe

This is a generic recipe for a homemade lasagna, it's easy to make but it can be simultaneously a very special meal.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the cheese mixture:

  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other:

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (uncooked or cooked, depending on the type)
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Preparation

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

    • In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef or sausage. Drain any excess fat.
    • Add the chopped onion and garlic. Sauté until softened.
    • Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20 minutes.

  2. Make the Cheese Mixture:

    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth.

  3. Assemble the Lasagna:

    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Layer 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese and then more meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce.
    • Finish with a final layer of noodles, meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella cheese on top.

  4. Bake:

    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray).
    • Bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
