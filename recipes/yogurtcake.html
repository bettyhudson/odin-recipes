Yogurt Cake Recipe

This is a nice recipe for when you-re craving that fluffy cake with a flourly sweet flavor but don't have much patience for a several-step elaborated cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of plain yogurt
  • 3 eggs
  • Half a tea cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 and a half tea cup of sugar
  • 2 tea cups full of flour
  • 1 tea spoon of baking powder

Preparation

  1. Blend the 3 eggs together with the yogurt, vegetable oil and sugar until smooth.
  2. Add the flour and the baking powder. Blend again.
  3. Transfer the better into a greased pan and put it in a pre-heated oven set for 180 degrees celsius for 30 minutes.
  4. Check if your cake is completely done and once it is, enjoy it with a cup of tea!
